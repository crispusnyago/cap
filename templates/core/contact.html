About Campus Accommodation Portal

Connecting students with safe, verified accommodation since 2025

Our Story

Born from a student's struggle

Campus Accommodation Portal was founded in 2025 by a group of university students who experienced firsthand the difficulty of finding safe, affordable accommodation near campus. We spent weeks walking around, calling landlords, and visiting questionable hostels.

We realized there had to be a better way. So we built CAP - a platform that connects students with verified hostels, making the search process simple, transparent, and stress-free.

Today, we serve over 5,000 students across 6 campuses and have 200+ trusted landlords on our platform. And we're just getting started!

Our Mission

"To ensure every student has access to safe, affordable, and quality housing near their campus."

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

How do I list my hostel?

Go to the Landlord Portal, create an account, and fill out the registration form. Upload photos of your property and we'll verify within 24 hours. Once verified, your hostel will appear in search results! You'll need to provide your personal details, hostel information, and Google Maps coordinates for accurate location mapping.

Is CampusAccommodationPortal free for students?

Absolutely! Browsing, searching, and contacting landlords is completely free for students. You only pay when you book through our secure platform. There are no hidden fees or subscription costs for students.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept MTN Mobile Money and Airtel Money for online bookings. Landlords also have the option to receive direct payments via bank transfer or mobile money. All payments are secured with SSL encryption.

How are hostels verified?

We require ID verification, physical visit or video call confirmation, and landlord contact verification. We also collect student reviews to maintain quality standards. Each hostel is personally checked by our team before being listed as "verified".

How do I get my Google Maps coordinates?

You can get your coordinates by right-clicking on your location in Google Maps and selecting "What's here?" The latitude and longitude will appear at the bottom of the screen. Copy these into the registration form so students can find your exact location.

Can I list my hostel for multiple campuses?

Yes! During registration, you can select which campus is nearest to your hostel. If your property is near multiple campuses, you can create separate listings for each or select the primary campus and mention proximity to others in the description.
